ABOUT PARTS AND LABOR

Parts and Labor is a Baltimore butcher shop and restaurant. Our Butcher Shop is locally sourced with pasture raised, whole animal butchery, and stocked with hand-picked provisions. The Restaurant serves the region's finest meats from the heath and twenty craft beers available on tap.
